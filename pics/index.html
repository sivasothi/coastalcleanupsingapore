ICCS 2002 Page

The International Coastal Cleanup, Singapore (ICCS) is in its 12th year in 2003. Thanks to all your support, we are still going strong! Last year, over 2,000 participants from schools and various organizations removed over 6.2 tonnes of trash from our beaches and mangroves!

 

 

70% of the trash collected comes from shoreline and recreational activities! Click here to view the 2002 summary.
