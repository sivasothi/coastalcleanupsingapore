The sites of this year's ICCS are broken up into 6 zones.

1. Northwest Zone
2. Northeast Zone
3. Pulau Ubin Zone

4. East Zone
5. West Zone
6. South Zone

Identify your zones and get to know your Zone Captain. Contact him/her if you have any questions regarding your zone.

Each site within the zone is also assigned a Site Captain, who will be in charge of the actual day operations. All Site Captains are to recce their site(s) to ensure that:

  • there is no construction activity on your site
  • you know your exact locations
  • you know where you will deposit trash – the trash collection point

Most of the sites have accompanying recce notes. Click on the links to view them.
