The International Coastal Cleanup, Singapore (ICCS) is going into its 13th year in 2004! Thanks to all your support, we are still going strong. Last year, over 2,000 participants from schools and various organizations removed over 74,000 pieces of trash, weighing over 6.4 tonnes from our beaches and mangroves.

 

Cigarette butts, plastic bags, and straws, yet again make the top 10 list! And more than half of trash collected came from land. Click here to view the 2003 consolidated results.
