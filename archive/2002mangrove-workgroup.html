International Coastal Cleanup Singapore (Mangroves) 2002
This is the 11th anniversary of the event in Singapore, and the 6th year of the Mangrove Cleanup. The Mangrove cleanup is coordinated by the NUS Raffles Museum of Biodiversity Research, with kind assistance from Sungei Buloh Wetlands Reserve.
